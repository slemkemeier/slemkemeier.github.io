About Us

The Nook is a non-profit retail store and gathering space in Ladue benefitting St. Louis Children’s Hospital. Volunteers sell clothing, unique gifts, and Missouri-made crafts. The space also is available for gatherings: you can host a puzzle night or your child’s next birthday party here. In addition, we offer classes and lectures provided by people in the community: sing-alongs, art projects and book readings for kids; crafting and learning for adults. We welcome people to help us create community! Come in for a cup of coffee, connect face-to-face with others in our community, let your kids see our fun space, read the paper, pick up a hostess gift - all in one spot. Come check out The Nook!


We are both a:



Retail Store

We sell apparel, home goods, unique gifts, and Missouri-made crafts.




and a...


Gathering Space

We provide a space for people to attend a class, host a children's party, book club, or puzzle night - your ideas are our assignments!
