About Us

The Nook is a non-profit retail store and gathering space in the heart of Ladue benefitting St. Louis Children’s Hospital. Volunteers sell clothing, unique gifts, and Missouri-made crafts. The space also is available for gatherings: you can host a puzzle night or your child’s next birthday party here. In addition, we offer classes on varied topics: learning calligraphy, succulent planting, travel experiences, etc. We welcome people to help us create community! Come in for a cup of coffee, connect face-to-face with others in our community, let your kids see our fun space, read the paper, pick up a hostess gift - all in one spot. Come check out The Nook!

Our Story

The goal of The Nook is to foster community. The original concept was similar to an old-fashioned general store where community could gather, exchange ideas, and purchase goods. The idea has morphed into a retail store and gathering space with all profits going to benefit St. Louis Children’s Hospital. Having been a grateful SLCH parent, served on the Development Board, and volunteered in the hospital schoolroom, I have seen the kindness, care and commitment which permeates Children’s. We are blessed it is a part of our community! Everyone bleeds, loves, laughs and cries in a hospital; all barriers and differences are left at the door. It is the definition of a community. The Nook is a continuation of that community which offers an additional way to support Children’s.

Coffee, newspapers, puzzles, and wifi are available in our cozy space. Lincoln Logs and a play area are on hand for kids whose mothers have an hour to while away between school schedules. Missouri crafts are featured alongside fashionable clothing. Embroidery and vinyl applications are available on site. We do not sell online; we think that old fashioned stores promote connection and community!

The Nook also is available as a gathering space. Want to host a book club but not clean your house? Need a space for your child’s next birthday party? Have you always wanted to learn calligraphy? Would your child like to lead a demonstration on how to make paracord bracelets? Care to host a puzzle night? Looking for a musical sing-along class for your child? Any of your ideas are welcome!

Volunteers staff the store while benefiting our city. People with talent to share teach classes. Community members are welcome to utilize the space for events. Whether a shopper, volunteer, craft-maker, teacher, or demonstrator, we appreciate your time, your talent, and your belief in this idea. We will not succeed without you!

The Nook will be opening on Clayton Road in Ladue November 2018. It will be a space formed by the community and for the community. Come be part of the fun!
